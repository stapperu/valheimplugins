## ElementalFalls
*For the most up-to-date and complete documentation, please visit the [official ElementalFalls page](https://valheim.cookiemilk.se/elementalfalls.html).*

### About
> Elemental Falls, Geysers, Puddles, Sludges, Rivers, Waterwheels.

### Features
> - Five elemental types: Water, Frost, Fire, Poison, and Lightning.
> - Multiple waterfall styles with different effects.
> - Geysers that erupt with elemental effects.
> - Puddles and sludges for ground-based effects.
> - Straight and turning rivers in water and lava variants.
> - Swimmable and non-swimmable river options.
> - Waterwheels in three different speeds.

### How to Use
> 1. Craft the ElementalFalls hammer at the Workbench.
> 2. After equipping the new Hammer you will find custom tabs in the Build Menu which are:
>
> - Geysers, Puddles, Sludges, and Rivers.<br>
> - All comes in Water, Frost, Fire, Poison, and Lightning element.
>
> 3. All pieces are interactable and deals their respective elemental damage.
