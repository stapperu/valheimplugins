You're ThirstyYou can now drink, store and purify water from water sources in Valheim!This is a complete hydration system. Your thirst drains constantly and not all water is safe. Boil your water to make sure that its safe to drink or your character will suffer from the consequences. Use containers to carry water and barrels to store it.The Thirst System :You now have the ability to drink from water sources in Valheim.Use containers to drink from.store water in barrels.Boil the water to make sure its safe.Water Containers :Waterskin - small leather pouch. Crafted early.Drinking Horn - holds less water than the waterskinWater Bucket - high capacity. Essential for boiling water and filling the barrels.Build Pieces - Barrel - Large container used for storing water at your base. Fill using buckets and other containers.Boiling Bucket - buildable after collecting boiling water from cauldron. Place on the ground to cool. pick it up for purified water.Cauldron Boiling - Boil water option appears in cauldrons menu. A live count down text will appear above cauldron when water is boiling. When the countdown is done, collect boiling water bucket and use your hammer to place it. Buffs and Debuffs : Hydrated - drinking clean water will improve movement speed and stamina regenDirty water sickness - drinking dirty water can cause your character to puke and can also reduce movement speed.Thirsty - if your thirst bar is low, your character will have reduced movement speed and decreased combat effectiveness.BOILING THE WATER REMOVES CONTAMINATIONHow to UseTo drink from water or fill containers from natural sources, press CTRL near waterUse containers to carry water and refill them at water sourcesBoiling water removes all contamination riskControlsAll keys are configurable in the BepInEx config.E near water - Drink directly or refill equipped container.E near water with bucket in inventory - Fill the bucket.E away from water - Empty your equipped container.G with waterskin or horn equipped - Drink from container.G at barrel with bucket - Pour bucket into barrel.G at barrel with waterskin or horn - Pour container into barrel or fill from barrel.CTRL near water to drink or fill containersE away from water to empty your equipped containerG with a container equipped to drink from itG at a barrel with a bucket to pour the bucket into the barrelG at a barrel with a waterskin or horn to fill from the barrelDifficulty ModesSet in the BepInEx config under DifficultyLevel.Easy - Slow drain, large containers, short sickness, fast boiling.Casual - Balanced default. Recommended starting point.Hard - Fast drain, small containers, long sickness, slow boiling.Custom - Set every value manually. Prevents presets from overwriting.Set the difficulty in the BepInEx config under DifficultyLevelEasy mode has slow thirst drain and short sicknessCasual is balanced and recommendedHard mode has fast drain and long sicknessCustom lets you set every value manuallyConfigurationA config file is created at BepInEx/config/marc.yourthirsty.cfg on first launchAdjust thirst duration, container capacities, boil and cool times, buff durations, sickness length, speed penalties, and moreSet DifficultyLevel to Custom before making manual changesA config file is created at BepInEx/config/marc.yourthirsty.cfg on first launch.
