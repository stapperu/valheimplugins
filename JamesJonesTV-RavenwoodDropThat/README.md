## Version 3.0.0 Update: Adds Essences, Souls and Dragon Hearts from the Elemental Dragons Mod.
- Adds a 5% chance for a Dragon Heart to drop from Fader.
- Adds a 2% chance for a Dragon Heart to drop form Moder.
- Adds a 1% chance for a Dragon Heart to drop from Wild Elemental Dragons.
- Adds a 1 in 10,000 chance for a Dragon Heart to drop from Drakes.
- Soul of Fire has a 25% chance to drop from Fader and 10% chance to drop from Svält.
- Soul of Frost has a 25% chance to drop from Moder, 10% chance to drop from Vrykolathas and 5% chance to drop from The Elder.
- Soul of Poison has a 25% chance to drop from The Queen, 10% chance to drop from Bonemass and 5% chance to drop from Asmodeus.
- Soul of Lightning has a 25% chance to drop from Yagluth and 10% chance to drop from Eikthyr.
- Souls will also drop at a 1% chance from a few select monsters related to the element that they may drop.
- Essences of Fire will always drop from Fader and Svält.
- Essences of Frost will always drop from Moder, Vrykolathas and The Elder.
- Essences of Poison will always drop from The Queen, Bonemass and Asmodeus.
- Essences of Lightning will always drop from Yagluth and Eikthyr.
- Elemental Essences will normally have a 20% chance to drop 1 essence from related monsters.
- Elemental Essences will normally have a 40% chance to drop 1-3 essences from related elite monster.
- Adds a 100% chance to drop 5 Serpent Scales and 5 Serpent Meat to all 4 Elemental Wild Dragons.
* A few monsters that deal multiple types of damage will have a chance to drop multiple types of essences and souls.
* Over half of all monsters will have a chance to drop an essence. Creatures that are able to be tamed have been left out.
