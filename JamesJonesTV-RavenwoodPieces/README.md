# This mod was made by MagicMike for the JamesJonesTV Server.

This mod includes:
- 1 Corewood Display Wall
- 1 Wood Double Door
- 1 Wood Ladder
- 4 Wooden Wall Pieces
- 6 Wooden Floor Pieces
- 3 Stone Castle Floor Pieces
- 1 Stone Castle Wall
- 1 Stone Castle Tower
- 1 Stone Castle Gateway
- 1 Massive Door to fit the Massive Stone Wall Arch.
- 10 Massive Stone Wall Pieces.
- 7 Massive Ice Stone Wall Pieces.
- 6 Massive Lava Stone Wall Pieces.
- 6 Massive Mossy Stone Wall Pieces.
- 7 Massive Black Stone Wall Pieces.
- 10 Massive Fortress Stone Wall Pieces.
- 1 Massive Gate and 1 Massive Door for the Stone Walls.
- 10 Signs for tameable creatures. Includes Monstrum/Wizardry tameable creatures.
- 5 Monster Protection Mushroom Lights. 10/25/50/75/100m Radius.
- 8 Hedges
- 7 Road Items
- 2 Long Bridge Walkways and a stairs item.
- 13 Buildings and a door to fit the buildings.
- 15 Indestructible larger versions of vanilla trees as buildable pieces. Also a new Willow Tree.

You will need to add Blue Mushrooms to your drop table for Blue, Green and Purple Lights recipes to work.
My drop that config mod can do this for you if you want. It will also add other finite drops or you can just create your own.
https://thunderstore.io/c/valheim/p/JamesJonesTV/DropThat_TrophyOreConfig/

The purpose of this mod is to work alongside MagicMike's CoreWoodExtras mod and add some things for my server they may not fit properly with his own mod.
