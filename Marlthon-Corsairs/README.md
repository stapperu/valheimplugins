## Corsairs Description
Corsairs – The Invaders Have Arrived!</br>
Corsairs introduces a new faction of enemies to Valheim: the fearsome Corsairs.</br>
They are invading all biomes, setting up camps filled with treasures and imprisoning slaves.</br>
Get ready to plunder, free the captives, and face a powerful boss for legendary rewards!</br>

<details>
<summary><b>CORSAIR CAMPS</b> (<i>click to expand</i>)</summary>
</br>
• Corsair camps will appear scattered across the game's 6 biomes: Meadows, Black Forest, Swamp, Mountain, Plains, and Ocean.</br>
• Each camp is filled with enemies and valuable treasures. Keep an eye out for the chests!</br>
• In the Plains and Ocean camps, you can take possession of the corsairs' boats after defeating them.</br>

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>NEW ENEMIES</b> (<i>click to expand</i>)</summary>
</br>
• Face different types of Corsairs, who get stronger as you progress through the biomes.</br>
• Discover the location of the boss island in the Plains and prepare for the final showdown against Rollo The looter.</br>
• By defeating him, you can loot his body for unique treasures, including Marlthon's Sword and Marlthon's Shield, powerful items for your journey.</br>

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>FREE AND RECRUIT SLAVES</b> (<i>click to expand</i>)</summary>
</br>
• Find slaves trapped in cages inside the camps.</br>
• By freeing them, they will become your loyal allies, helping you fight against enemies.</br>
• Don't forget to feed them! They eat CookedMeat, CookedDeerMeat, and FishCooked.</br>

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>HOW TO PLAY:</b> (<i>click to expand</i>)</summary>
</br>
<B>How do I find the boss Rollo?</B></br>
• In the Corsair camps in the Plains, you will find a special Vegvisir that will reveal the location of the boss's island on your map.</br>
</br>
<B>How do I summon the boss Rollo?</B></br>
• You need 10 "Bronze Padlocks". When you arrive at the boss island, find a large soup cauldron. Throw the 10 padlocks into the soup to anger Rollo and start the battle.</br>
</br>
<B>Where do I find the Bronze Padlocks?</B></br>
• "Bronze Padlocks" are dropped exclusively by Corsairs found in the Plains camps. You can also find them in the chests within these camps.</br>
</br>
<B>How do the freed slaves work?</B></br>
• After being freed from the cages, they become your followers and will attack nearby enemies. Remember to feed them Cooked Meat, they will eat any cooked vanilla meat.</br>
</br>
<B>How to free the slaves?</B></br>
• You need to use the pickaxe on the cage that imprisons the slave. Each biome and its respective cage requires different pickaxes.</br>

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>HOW TO ADD LOCATIONS TO OLD MAP</b> (<i>click to expand</i>)</summary>
</br>
<B>Always create a backup of your world before installing or updating this mod. Use at your own risk.</B></br>
• Install Corsairs mod.</br>
• Install the <a href="https://valheim.thunderstore.io/package/JereKuusela/Upgrade_World">UPGRADE WORLD</a> mod and its dependencies.</br>
• Start the game as administrator.</br>
• Open the console and type:</br>
- <b>corsairs</b> to spawn Corsair camps on your old map.</br>
- <b>corsairs_remove</b> to remove Corsair camps on your map.</br>
• Wait for the process to finish, it takes between 20 or 30 seconds.</br>
• On dedicated servers you need to download the map and do the steps above in singleplayer</br>
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>MANUAL INSTALL</b> (<i>click to expand</i>)</summary>
</br>
• Mod is required on Server for Config Sync (this is still in development).</br>
• Download the latest copy of Bepinex per author's instructions.</br>
• Place the Corsairs.dll inside of the "Bepinex\plugins\" folder.</br>
</details>
